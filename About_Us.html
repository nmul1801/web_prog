About Us

In the heart of the city, Nick, Jacob and Adhvith are all food enthusiasts who share a common concern - the possibility of being poisoned by the food we eat. Poisonings are becoming more frequent in the city and we've decided to do something about it.

We come up with a unique approach to detecting poison - we taste the dishes ourselves, using only our senses and intuition. We believe that the human body has an innate ability to detect danger, and we train ourselves to identify any suspicious flavors or textures in the food.

At first, people are skeptical of our service. They wonder how we can detect poisons without the aid of chemical techniques. But as we start to gain a reputation for our accuracy, more and more people begin to trust us.

Our business grows, and we start to receive requests from local restaurants and caterers. We arrive at the restaurant and taste every dish before it is served to the customers. If we detect anything suspicious, we inform the chef and recommend the dish not be served.

Our unique approach is not without challenges. We have to endure numerous taste tests, and we have to train ourselves to identify subtle differences in flavors and textures. But we persist, and our reputation grows.

As our business expands, we realize that we need to train more people to help us. We recruit food enthusiasts like ourselves and teach them our unique approach to detecting poison. We also develop a training program for restaurant staff, teaching them how to detect poison in their own kitchens.

Over time, our business becomes known throughout the city as a trusted and reliable service. We have successfully created a new method for detecting poison, one that relies on human intuition and experience rather than chemical techniques. We make the city a safer place to eat, and we inspire a new generation of food enthusiasts to follow in our footsteps.
