About Us

We believe that the human body has an innate ability to detect danger, and we train ourselves to identify any suspicious flavors or textures in the food.

They wonder how we can detect poisons without the aid of chemical techniques. We endure numerous taste tests and train ourselves to identify subtle differences in flavors and textures.

At first, people are skeptical of our service. But now we have chefs, star athletes and even presidents flocking to us to safe-test their food!

We make the city a safer place to eat, and we inspire a new generation of food enthusiasts to follow in our footsteps.
