Village Spirit Shop

At our liquor store, we place great importance on providing top-notch customer service to our valued patrons. We believe that every customer should receive personalized attention and care, and we strive to make sure that their experience with us is nothing short of exceptional. In addition, we understand that price is a crucial factor when it comes to purchasing spirits, which is why we are committed to offering a wide selection of high-quality products at affordable, low prices. Whether you're looking for your favorite brand of whiskey, a unique craft beer, or a special bottle of wine, we've got you covered. Shop with us and experience the difference of our outstanding customer service and unbeatable prices.

  • 2023
    The Crash
    It was a typical day at our liquor store until a car crashed into our building, causing significant damage. We were forced to close for a period of time while repairs were made and it was a challenging experience for our business. The cost of repairs and renovations was significant, and it was a difficult financial burden for us to bear. We were overwhelmed by the outpouring of support we received from the community during this time, which helped us to persevere through the challenges. Though the experience was tough, we're proud of how we came together as a team and the resilience we showed as a business. Today, our store is thriving once again.
