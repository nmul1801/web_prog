Join Our Team!

If you're passionate about food safety and are up for a challenge, then our company may have the perfect career opportunity for you. A wide variety of positions are open to join our company. From food taster to HR manager, if you want to be a part of detecting deadly dished, we urge you to join our team today!

Our Positions Include:

  • Chief Food Taster - As the lead food tester, this person would be responsible for picking up the slack if another employee is late or unable to test
  • Food Safety Specialist - This person would handle the legal troubles that come with our company. Handling claims of employee safety is crucial to the sustainability of our business
  • HR Manager - This person would be responsible for managing all aspects of human resources. Vetting out wannabe food tasters is a must. This also includes recruiting, hiring, and training new employees
  • Research and Development Scientist - This person would be responsible for creating new food tasting and testing methods
