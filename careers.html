Join Our Team!

If you're passionate about food safety and are up for a challenge, then our company may have the perfect career opportunity for you. We specialize in testing potentially deadly meals to determine whether they are safe for consumption. As a member of our team, you will have the opportunity to work with a dynamic group of experts dedicated to ensuring that the food we eat is safe and free from harmful contaminants. We are looking for individuals with a background in food science, biology, chemistry, or related fields who have a strong attention to detail and are committed to upholding rigorous safety standards. If you're interested in joining us, apply for one of our open positions today!

Our Positions Include:

  • Chief Food Taster - As the lead food tester, this person would be responsible for overseeing all food testing activities and ensuring that the highest standards of safety and quality are met.
  • Food Safety Specialist - This person would work closely with the food tasters to develop and implement food safety protocols and procedures, and to ensure that all testing is conducted in accordance with regulatory standards.
  • HR Manager - This person would be responsible for managing all aspects of human resources, including recruiting, hiring, and training new employees, managing employee benefits and compensation, and ensuring that the company is in compliance with all employment laws and regulations.
  • Research and Development Scientist - This person would be responsible for developing new food testing methods and technologies, and for conducting research on emerging food safety issues and trends.
